Online Assessment Guidelines


  • The examination will comprise of Objective type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • There will be Three different levels. Each level contains of Ten questions and each question carries One mark.
  • Total time alloted for the exam (including all the levels) is 30 mins, which is 10 mins alloted for each level.
  • There will be NO NEGATIVE MARKING for the wrong answers.
  • The Time of the examination begins only when the ‘START EXAM’ button is pressed.
  • The Time remaining is shown in the Right Top Corner of the screen.
  • The timer automatically stops when the time limit is over OR alternatively if examinee finishes the exam before time he can quit by pressing the ‘Submit Test’ button.
  • Make sure that your laptop/Desktop is fully charged.
  • Make sure you have a good and stable internet connection.
